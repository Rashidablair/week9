Delicious foods

Jamaica is know for its beautiful beaches and delicious foods. Some of the most popular dishes are jerk chicken, beef patties, oxtail with rice and peas, jerk pork, curried goat etc.

Food Location
1 Jerk Chicken Scotchie's Jerk Center in Montego Bay
2 Beef Patties Tastee or Juici Beef Patty shop
3 Seafood Sharkie's Seafood Restaurant
